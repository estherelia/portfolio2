ABOUT

Esther is an illustrator with a focus in writing and interpreting both the writings of herself and others into images. She uses painting and digital processes to create editorial illustrations, books, and fine art pieces.

Her writing has been chosen as a competition winner for the CCA Humble Pie Publication, an excerpt taken from a larger publication exploring the complexities of abuse and divorce -- totalling eight handmade books. She has been featured in group and solo shows in Ireland and the US which display her paintings, writings, and handmade clothing exploring themes of identity as the grandchild of refugees. She is an active member of MENA -- the Middle-Eastern art club at her college, and passionately explores her ethnic heritage through art and comics. In addition, she was chosen to be in the CCA Honors Club and Show -- based on merit both academically and artistically. In the future she would love to explore her interest in human stories in the field of journalistic illustration.
